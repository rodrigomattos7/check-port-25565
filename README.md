## 🚀 What is Port 25565?

Port **25565** is the default port used by Minecraft servers.
If this port is closed, players won't be able to connect to your server from outside your network.

## ❗ Port 25565 Closed? Here's Why

If your port is closed, it could be caused by:

* Router not configured (port forwarding missing)
* Firewall blocking the connection
* Incorrect internal IP address
* ISP restrictions

---

## 🔧 How to Open Port 25565

To fix the issue:

* Enable **port forwarding** on your router
* Forward port **25565** to your local IP
* Allow the port in your firewall (Windows/Linux)
* Restart your router and server
